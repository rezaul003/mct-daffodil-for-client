ABOUT MCT

The MULTIMEDIA & CREATIVE TECHNOLOGY department at Daffodil International University combines creativity with technology, offering comprehensive courses in animation, graphic design, video editing, and interactive media. With hands-on training, industry exposure, and a focus on innovation, it prepares students for dynamic careers in media, design, and entertainment, emphasizing project-based learning and technical excellence. The department fosters collaboration and equips students with skills to thrive in creative industries.

Our Facilities

Our department offers advanced labs, creative studios, industry exposure, skilled faculty, and hands-on training.
